## Jobs

- Sets a number of pods to completion.
- Managed by the Job Controller.
    - Provides out of the box retries, restarts, clean and auto kill long-run jobs.
- Can run pods in parallel.

## Cron Jobs

- Much like Jobs but run on a schedule.
